Our mission is to give you a guideline for your health.

Welcome to Dico Care, your trusted partner in diabetic management through personalized workout and diet plans. At Dico Care, we recognize that managing diabetes requires a personalized approach. workout schedule

brisk walking

It increased cardiovascular and pulmonary (heart and lung) fitness. It reduced risk of heart disease and stroke. It improved management of conditions such as hypertension, diabetes.

cycling

Cycling is an aerobic exercise that gets the heart pumping and the blood flowing, which can help improve overall health and well-being. It helps improve insulin sensitivity and lower blood glucose levels.

swimming

It help to strengthen muscles and improve coordination. It help you manage your weight . It help to look after your blood pressure and cholesterol and keep your heart healthy.

weight lifting

It helps support healthy weight loss by building lean muscle tissue that reduces your total body fat percentage. It reduce the risk of type 2 diabetes and cardiovascular disease.

yoga

It help to improve the sensitivity of β-cells to glucose, thereby improving insulin secretion, and It increase the blood supply to the muscle and muscle relaxation, thereby improving glucose uptake.

team sports

Exercise makes insulin work better in the body, which helps people with diabetes keep their blood sugar levels in a healthier range. It makes your body more sensitive to insulin, which helps manage your diabetes.

Aerobic dance

It increases insulin sensitivity, which helps the cells use any available insulin to take up glucose from the blood . It increased muscular strength, endurance and motor fitness.

pilates

It help build lean muscle mass and aid in the management of blood sugar and insulin levels . It increased muscle strength and tone, lower back, hips and buttocks balanced muscular strength on both sides of your body.

Calisthenics

It makes your body more sensitive to insulin which helps manage your diabetes. Physical activity also helps control blood sugar levels and lowers your risk of heart disease and nerve damage.

Resistance band exercises

Weights are not the only tool you can use to strengthen your muscles. You can also perform a wide variety of strengthening activities with resistance bands.
